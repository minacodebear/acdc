About AC/DC

Facts about AC/DC

- The band is named after a label on Angus (lead guitarist) and Malcolm Young's (rhythm guitarist) sister's sewing machine that read " AC/DC", meaning alternating current/ direct current

- Angus Young, the lead guitarist, always wears a school uniform in concerts but not all the time. He has tried other costumes, such as Spider-Man, Zorro, and a gorilla

- Researchers are using AC/DC's music to help combat cancer. They found that the vibrations from their songs can kill tumor cells

- Brian Johnson (lead singer) is also a car racing enthusiant. He competed in the 2012 Silverstone Classic Celebrity race

- Angus Young played his electric guitar while his amp (amplifier) was on fire during a concert in 1977. He didn't notice the flames until a roadie put them out

Band Members History

Bon Scott (lead singer, joined in 1974) was AC/DC's first lead singer. Bon Scott died in February 19, 1980.Brian Johnson joined AC/DC in April 1, 1980. Later, Brian Johnson was forced to leave AC/DC in 2016 because he suffered severe hearing loss in a concert Rock Or Bust. If he stayed he would have been deaf. During the concert, Rock Or Bust, Axl Rose filled in for Brian Johnson. After that, Axl Rose left in 2017. Brian Johnson rejoined AC/DC in 2018 to make the album Power Up.

Colin Burgess was AC/DC's original drummer. Later in February 1975, Phil Rudd joined AC/DC. Phil Rudd later left in 1983. After Rudd left, Simon Wright joined. Simon Wright left AC/DC in 1989. Later, Chris Slade joined AC/DC in 1989. Slade left AC/DC in 2020. After Slade, Phil Rudd rejoined AC/DC.

AC/DC's first rhythm guitarist was Malcolm Young. He was also the co-finder of AC/DC. Malcolm Young died in 2017. After, Stevie Young was the rhythm guitarist. AC/DC's first bass guitarist was Larry Van Kriedt. Larry Van Kriedt left AC/DC in 1975. Mark Evans replaced Larry Van Kriedt. Mark Evans left in 1977. After Mark Evans, Cliff Williams joined. Later, Cliff Williams retired from AC/DC in 2016. In the August of 2018, Williams returned to AC/DC. Angus Young is AC/DC's lead guitarist and founder. Malcolm and Angus Young are the songwriter of AC/DC.
